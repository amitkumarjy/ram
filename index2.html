About Heart Disease

Heart disease refers to a range of conditions that affect the heart. These conditions include:

  • Coronary artery disease
  • Heart rhythm problems (arrhythmias)
  • Heart defects you're born with (congenital heart defects)
  • Heart valve disease

Heart disease is a leading cause of death worldwide. Risk factors include:

  • High blood pressure
  • High cholesterol
  • Smoking
  • Obesity
  • Diabetes

For more information, please consult a healthcare provider.
